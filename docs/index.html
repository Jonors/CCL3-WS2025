Overview

MoviLog is a mobile application developed with Kotlin and Android Studio. It serves as a personal cinema diary, allowing users to log, rate, and organize their movie history and future watchlists.

Use Case

Keep track of movie backlogs, maintain a personal rating system, and analyze viewing habits over time.

Target Users

Cinephiles and casual viewers who want a structured way to manage their movie consumption.
